Sound for Games




While one could say that sound design for film or visual media should be similar to sound for video games, it is fair to highlight and value the different aspects that make the latter perhaps more complex. Unlike film or conventional visual media, video games live in a Non-linear world where outcomes are always different and change depending on the player's actions. This aspect completely changes how to approach sound-wise because the sound designer and music composer has now to think about different outcomes, themes, and ways to connect with what the player is experiencing.
